simplicity speaks volumes

I like to keep things simple. In life and in design.


An elementary teacher turned UX/UI designer. I’ve been designing academic lessons for 13 years, now I’m designing digital solutions and experiences. I’m all about keeping things simple and staying focused on the main goals. What is it the users are trying to do? Is the design clean and modern to attract and effectively guide users? Clean, modern and uncluttered design is my jam. Efficient, simple and pleasant user experiences are my end game. When I’m not thinking about UX/UI design, I’m probably trying a new baking recipe looking for the perfect chocolate chip cookie recipe (Found it? Let me know.). I can also be found working on a DIY project. My most favorite project? Making my son’s mobile!
